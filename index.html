Our Story

Silver Dairy was purchased by Jason Smith in 2009. His passion for serving others motivated him to create a local Farmington staple centered around families and great customer service. Silver Dairy has become a place where people from all over the Farmington area can meet 'to take a break from the business of the world and enjoy a delicious treat.

Our menu is varied and the service is unmatched. If you're looking for a fun and enjoyable Farmington experience, stop by to visit us today!

Who We Are

The Silver Dairy has been a staple in the Farmington community for many years. Jason Smith bought this business in 2009 with one goal in mind: providing delicious ice cream and outstanding customer service to the greater Farmington area. Thanks to our experience and dedication, we've managed to serve our loyal customers for many years. Providing tasty classic and new menu items, supporting local schools, and adapting to the times, we'd like to say that we've become a Farmington favorite.
